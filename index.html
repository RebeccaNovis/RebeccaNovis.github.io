Star Bay



Star Bay is a 2D platformer I developed in Unity. In the game, you navigate the wreckage of your base trying to reach the safty of the Star Bay. Along the way, you are expected to collect stars, avoid meteors, and stay continue moving before the wrackage of the base falls away from you.

This was my first video game I developed independently in Unity. Through this project, I learned much about using Unity and programming in C#.

Pursuit of Time



Persuit of Time is an interactive comic developed in Unity. Persuit of Time is about the bi-linear flow of time, and the journey of Janus, a teenage girl, as she explores her past and future in order to avoid a grim fate

This project was the result of a collaborative effort to create an interactive comic about a prompt givent to us by our professor: the bilinear flow of time. Most of my contributions were in the conceptualization of the actual story and organization of our group. I also created some of the art and dialouge in the comic. Working on Persuit of Time with the rest of my group was a really fun and valuable experience, allowing me to further push my creativity and communication skills.
