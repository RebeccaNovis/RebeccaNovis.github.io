Star Bay

Fall 2021



Skills Used

Unity
C#
Sprite creation

Star Bay is a 2D platformer I developed in Unity. In the game, you navigate the wreckage of your base trying to reach the safety of the Star Bay. Along the way, you are expected to collect stars, avoid meteors, and stay continue moving before the wrackage of the base falls away from you.

This was my first video game I developed independently in Unity. Through this project, I learned much about using Unity and programming in C#. Furthermore, with this being my first big project in Unity, I only knew some of the very basics of Unity and C# going in, so working on this game really helped me stretch my skills and get comfortable working with a new skill. I faced many challenges when trying to impliment new features, but I was always able to find a way to acomplish what I wanted.

Eventually I ran out of time to work on Star Bay, but I had still accomplished everything that I had initially hoped I would, and even a bit more.

Pursuit of Time

Spring 2022



Skills Used

Unity
C#
Adobe XD
Illustration
Writing
Conceptualization
Communication
Teamwork

Persuit of Time is an interactive comic developed in Unity. Persuit of Time is about the bi-linear flow of time, and the journey of Janus, a teenage girl, as she explores her past and future in order to avoid a grim fate

This project was the result of a collaborative effort to create an interactive comic about a prompt givent to us by our professor: the bilinear flow of time. Most of my contributions were in the conceptualization and writing of the story, as well as maintaining the organization of our group. I also created some of the art and dialouge in the comic, and helped troubleshoot some code. Working on Persuit of Time with the rest of my group was a really fun and valuable experience, allowing me to further push my creativity and communication skills.

The Legends' Items

Fall 2022



Skills Used

HTML
CSS
JavaScript
d3.js
node.js
MySQL

This website displays my statistical analysis of how items are implemented in The Legend of Zelda series. I gathed information on every item used across the Zelda franchise, such as what game they first appeared in, and how many games each item appeared in across the series. Then, I displayed this data using data visualizations.

This was my first big broject utilizing what I had learned about HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Node.js, and MySQL. I gathered and analyzed all of the data myself, then displayed them using a variety of data visualizations, the majority of which were made with d3.js. I also began using MySQL databases to hold my data for some visualizations.
